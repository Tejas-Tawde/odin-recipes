Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a crowd-pleasing choice for busy weeknights and fun weekend dinners alike. But you don't have to rely on restaurants or takeout for top-notch pizza. Read on for tips on how to make your own pizza at home.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Pizza Sauces
  • Pizza Toppings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oven.
  2. Form the pizza dough and place it on a peel dusted with a little flour or cornmeal.
  3. Slip the pizza onto the hot pizza stone.
  4. After 5 minutes of baking, check the pizza.
  5. The pizza is done when the cheese is melted to a medium-to-dark brown.
